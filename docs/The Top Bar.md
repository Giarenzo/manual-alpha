The top bar is a reference menu that you will see in all pages of the user interface. Its purpose is to allow you to have all the most relevant pieces of information and functionalities always readily available

# **Main Menu (Dropdown)**

- **Accessing the Main Menu**: Click on the three horizontal lines (hamburger icon) located at the top-left corner of the dashboard.
- **Navigating Options**: The dropdown menu provides access to various system functionalities such as event management, live camera feeds, statistics, settings, and more. This menu replicates the one you see on the main page. 

# BCMS Logo

- The BCMS Ventur Logo is a link to the main page

# **Weather Information (VGHS number)**

- **Viewing Weather Data**: The string of numbers and characters at the top of the dashboard, is the VGHS number displaying current weather information at the installation site.
- **Detailed Weather Reports**: Click on the weather string to access more detailed weather reports and forecasts, crucial for bird activity analysis.

# Profile

- The stylised person icon allows access to your user profile and preferences. It displays in writing what profile is currently logged in. Clicking it will reveal a drop-down menu revealing the following elements:
    - My account: here you can edit your account (photo, name, email) and see a log of recent activity.
    - Preferences: here you can choose your display mode (dark/light) and edit your notification preferences (pop-up, leave them and the signal off)
    - Logout: click to log out of the system

# **System Status**

- **Monitoring System Health**: The system status indicator is located at the top-right corner.
    - If the light is green, everything is working correctly.
    - If the light is yellow or red, something is not working.
    - Clicking on the icon will reveal a drop-down menu displaying all parts of the installed system with a marker on their status, indicating precisely what is not working as expected and allowing for action. You may see different colour-coded icons, such as a green tick (all good) a red triangle (critical error) or a yellow triangle (non-critical error). In some cases, hovering over a status icon will reveal additional information.
- **Compiling Tickets**: In case of a red status, click on the indicator to access the ticketing system and report the issue for assistance.

# Screen Icon

- The screen icon allows you to enter full-screen mode

# **Eye Icon**

Clicking on the eye icon reveals a menu with the most recent events. 

- **Daily Events Summary**: The eye icon provides a summary of the daily events, including the number of bird detections and their classifications, as well as event date, time and sector.
- **Accessing Event Details**: Click on the event count to view detailed information about each event.
- **Camera Icon**: clicking on the icon will take you to the video viewer and show the recording of the event
- **Edit Icon**: clicking on this icon allows users to edit details of the event manually.
