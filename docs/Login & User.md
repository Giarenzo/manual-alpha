# Login and User Management

This section provides detailed instructions on how to log in to the BCMS system, including accepting the Terms & Conditions, accessing and updating your personal profile, and setting preferences for display, signals, and notifications. These steps ensure that users can securely access the system, customize their experience, and stay informed about important events and alerts within the BCMS environment.

[https://app.sharefable.com/embed/demo/demo-login-b3df31iiujfq2irx](https://app.sharefable.com/embed/demo/demo-login-b3df31iiujfq2irx)

**Accept Terms & Conditions**

1. **Navigate to the Login Page**: Open your web browser and enter the BCMS system URL.
2. **Enter Credentials**: On the login page, enter your username and password in the respective fields.
3. **Review Terms & Conditions**:
    - Before accessing the system, you will be prompted to review the Terms & Conditions.
    - Carefully read through the document to understand your responsibilities and the system’s usage policies.
4. **Accept Terms & Conditions**:
    - Check the box indicating that you agree to the Terms & Conditions.
    - Click on the “Accept” button to proceed.

**Access Personal Profile**

5. **Login to the System**: After accepting the Terms & Conditions, you will be redirected to the main dashboard.
6. **Navigate to Profile**:
    - Click on your username or profile icon located at the top-right corner of the dashboard.
    - Select “Profile” from the dropdown menu.
7. **View and Edit Profile**:
    - In the profile section, you can view your personal information, such as name, email, and role.
    - To update your information, click on the “Edit” button, make the necessary changes, and save them by clicking “Save” or “Update.”

**Set Preferences for Display, Signals, and Notifications**

8. **Access Preferences**:
    - Within the Profile section, look for the “Preferences” tab or section.
    - Click on “Preferences” to open the settings page.
9. **Display Preferences**:
    - Adjust the layout and theme of the dashboard to suit your needs.
    - Options may include light/dark mode, font size, and dashboard widget arrangement.
10. **Signal Preferences**:
    - Configure how the system signals alerts and notifications.
    - Options might include sound alerts, visual pop-ups, or email notifications for specific events.
11. **Notification Preferences**:
    - Set your preferences for receiving notifications.
    - You can choose to receive notifications for various events such as bird detections, system updates, or maintenance alerts.
    - Specify the method of notification, such as email, SMS, or in-system alerts.
12. **Save Preferences**:
    - After configuring your preferences, click on the “Save” button to apply the changes.
    - Ensure that your preferences are updated and active.

By following these steps, users can effectively log in, manage their personal profiles, and set their display, signal, and notification preferences, ensuring a customized and efficient experience with the BCMS system.
