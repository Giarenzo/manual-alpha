The Live Camera Controls section provides detailed guidelines for accessing, controlling, and utilizing live camera feeds within the BCMS system. This functionality is essential for real-time monitoring of bird activities and ensuring the safety and efficiency of operations.

# **Accessing and controlling the live camera feeds**

## **Navigating to Live Feeds**:

- Access the live camera feeds from the dashboard by clicking on the “Live Cams” section.
- Alternatively, you can select a camera icon from the live map.

## **Viewing Live Feeds**:

- Click on the desired camera feed to open it in a larger view.
- On the left side, you see a livestream of what the camera sees, in the column on the right side, you see the following controls.
- The drop-down menu allows you to select the resolution for the camera livestream.
- The lever icon allows you to enter PTZ (Pan, Tilt, Zoom) mode. This means that you have direct control of the camera orientation. Selecting this option prompts a pop-up warning informing you that as long as the camera is in PTZ more, its bird detection functionality will be disabled.
- Clicking on the film icon will allow users to review a recording of their choice.

# **Manual Control **

## **Enabling Manual Control**:

- To take manual control of the camera, click on the “Action Bar” at the top of the live feed window.
- Select “Confirm” to switch the camera from automatic scanning to manual control mode.

## **Zooming In and Out**:

- Use the zoom controls to focus on specific areas. This can be done by:
    - Using the zoom slider on the screen.
    - Using the mouse scroll wheel if supported.
    - Drawing a box in the image to trigger the camera to zoom to the correct position.

## **Panning the Camera**:

- To pan the camera, click and drag the video feed in the desired direction.
- You can move the camera horizontally (left and right) and vertically (up and down) to follow bird movements or focus on specific areas.

# **Autofocus Settings**

## **Adjusting Focus**:

- The camera is equipped with autofocus to maintain a clear image.
- If necessary, you can manually adjust the focus by clicking the “Focus” button and selecting the area you want to focus on.

## **Returning to Automatic Mode**:

- To revert the camera to its pre-set scanning pattern, click the “Close” button on the action bar.
- This will disengage manual control and return the camera to its automatic scanning mode.
